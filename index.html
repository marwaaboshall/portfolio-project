BluBlye

Welcome to BluBlye's city!
BluBlye's city is overrun by lots of creepies, there's only one thing left to do - survive! As the lone survivor of a creepies apocalypse, it's up to you to battle massive number of creepies in a never ending fight for survival!
How long can you hold out? BluBlye combines the timeless appeal of side scrolling shoot'em up games with some lively cute animations!

FEATURES:

  • Defend yourself with different weapons found through your continuous game-play!.
  • Simple gameplay, with more depth than meets the eye. Manage your movements carefully, or become the dinner of creepies.
  • Experience the day/night cycles of BluBlye's city, according to your current time zone!.
